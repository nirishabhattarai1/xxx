Integrity, Accountability and Dependability

At Clever Choice we hold ourselves to highest professional standards and we treat each piece of flooring that we sell as if it was being installed in our own homes. Whether it’s a single board or a whole house of flooring, nothing is installed unless its manufactured perfectly.

Integrity

Integrity is the cornerstone of our company. We work to build trust with all our customers by conducting our business in an honest manner, being truthful and ethical in all matters.

Accountability

The accountability that we have instilled at an organizational level ensures that each and every staff member is accountable for their actions and as such when you deal with someone from Clever Choice you are dealing with someone who is passionate and values their work.

Dependability

You need to be confident that you can rely on the team at Clever Choice to deliver a solution when it’s needed. We go above and beyond almost on a daily basis and our customers know that the team at Clever Choice can be relied on.

Our focus on quality first has gained us recognition as the benchmark to which other suppliers aspire. We are selective with the flooring we offer, as not all flooring is made to the same standards.
The ‘Clever’ brand has been specified and installed in Government, major residential, and commercial construction projects throughout Australia. We have been relied on for our:

  • Super quality flooring
  • Most stable, durable and
  • Value for money

Clever Choice products are designed and manufactured to continue to perform over their recommended lifetimes and are fully covered by structural and finish warranties.
Our resellers are selected for their professional customer service, excellent product knowledge, and after-sales support ensuring satisfaction from your new flooring experience.
